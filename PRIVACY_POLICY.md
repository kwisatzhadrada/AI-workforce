## 1. What we collect

**Account data**: your name, email address, and login credentials
(handled by Supabase Auth; we never see your raw password).

**Organization data**: your company name, industry, and team details you
provide, plus every real business record the Service creates on your
behalf — prospects found, emails drafted and sent, replies received,
meetings booked, and revenue you record.

**Connected account data**: when you connect Gmail, we store an OAuth
refresh token (not your Google password) that lets the Service send email
and check replies on your behalf. When you connect HubSpot or Hunter.io,
we store the access token/API key you provide.

**Usage data**: login timestamps and frequency, which features you use,
and platform activity — used to compute your own account's health
and to answer basic "how many people use this" questions in aggregate.
We do not sell or share individual usage data with third parties.

**Billing data**: handled by Stripe. We store your subscription status
and plan, not your card number — Stripe holds that directly.

## 2. Who we share data with

- **Google (Gmail API)** — to send email and check replies through your
  connected account, per your own OAuth authorization.
- **HubSpot** and **Hunter.io** — only if and when you connect them,
  using the credentials you provide.
- **OpenAI and/or Anthropic** — prospect names/companies and campaign
  context are sent to whichever AI model provider is configured, solely
  to draft outreach email and classify replies. We do not control these
  providers' own data retention; consult their own policies for how long
  they retain API request content.
- **Stripe** — for billing, subscription status, and payment processing.
- We do not sell your data to advertisers or data brokers.

## 3. Why we collect it

Every piece of data above exists to run your actual sales campaign —
finding prospects, drafting and sending outreach, tracking replies and
meetings — or to operate the business itself (billing, support,
security). Nothing is collected "just in case."

## 4. Data retention

active. If you close your account, contact [SUPPORT EMAIL ADDRESS] to
request deletion; we will remove your data within a reasonable period,
except where retention is required for legal or billing recordkeeping.

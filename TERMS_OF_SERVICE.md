## 1. What this service is

AI Workforce ("the Service," "we," "us") is a B2B sales outreach platform.
When you connect your own Gmail account, the Service finds prospects,
drafts outreach email using AI, and — only after you approve each
batch — sends that email through your own connected Gmail account. It
also tracks replies, meetings, and revenue you record.

## 3. Connected third-party accounts

You may connect:
- **Gmail** (Google) — to send outreach email and check for replies, on
  your explicit authorization via Google's OAuth consent screen.
- **HubSpot** — to sync contacts to your CRM, using a Private App access

- **Hunter.io** — to find and enrich prospect contact information, using

You are responsible for complying with each of these providers' own
terms of service, and for the content of any email sent through your
connected Gmail account.

- Obtaining any consent required to contact the prospects you target.
- Your own Gmail account's standing with Google — the Service provides
  send-rate safety controls, but cannot guarantee Google will not flag or
